Review Flags — CSSI Report Discrepancies
✗

St. Charles — summary sheet does not foot

CSSI's summary sheet prints a total of (1,101,219.87), but its own 18 line items sum to (1,762,893.13) — a difference of $661,673.26 in deductions. Every unit schedule is internally consistent; only the printed total is wrong. Workbook foots from the asset rows.

✗

Macedonia — summary sheet does not foot

Printed total (148,277.87) vs. line items that sum to (220,443.87). Workbook foots row-true to (220,443.79); the remaining $0.08 is penny rounding inside CSSI's own Grand Total lines, disclosed in the workbook notes.

✗

Coral Village — Office unit excluded from printed total

The summary sheet lists an Office unit at +11,625.28 (with no EIN shown) but the printed total of (277,137.61) excludes it. All eleven listed units sum to (265,512.33), which is what the workbook uses. Confirm with CSSI whether Office is in or out of the filing.

▲

River Garden — Building 15 Regular Tax page missing

The PDF contains a duplicate of Building 16's Regular Tax page where Building 15's should be. Building 15's AMT schedule exists and ties to the summary sheet (22,947.08); it is used for both sides, flagged in red on that tab.

▲

Year of change — 1/1/2024 vs 1/1/2025

Every Form 3115 face page states a tax year of change beginning 1/1/2025, while the attached statements and all 481(a) schedules say 1/1/2024 (FYE 12/31/2024). CSSI's cover letter references the 2025 tax return. Preparer to confirm the intended year of change before filing; noted on every workbook Summary.
